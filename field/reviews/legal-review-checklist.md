# Independent legal review checklist

- Identify every source, transit, hosting, and destination jurisdiction.
- Verify ownership and written authorization for every endpoint and network.
- Review computer-misuse, interception, telecommunications, encryption,
  export-control, sanctions, provider-contract, privacy, and data-retention
  requirements that may apply.
- Confirm that no third-party network or service is tested without explicit
  written authorization.
- Record geographic, temporal, operational, and publication restrictions.
- Identify the responsible legal reviewer and signed decision reference.
- Require re-review after material protocol, infrastructure, jurisdiction, or
  study-scope changes.
