# Independent ethics review checklist

- Confirm that the initial deployment uses only synthetic payloads and named,
  researcher-owned endpoints.
- Determine whether institutional review or a documented exemption is needed.
- Exclude human participants, user traffic, third-party service traffic,
  identifiers, contact graphs, and content collection.
- Review risks to operators, bystanders, infrastructure providers, and people
  in high-risk jurisdictions.
- Review data minimization, retention, deletion, access controls, incident
  response, stop rules, and publication risk.
- Confirm that the operation and duration caps are proportionate and that the
  safety contact can stop the study immediately.
- Record residual risks and the exact approved scope. Any scope expansion
  requires a new review.
